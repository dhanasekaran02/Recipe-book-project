Ingredients

Rice - 3 Cups
Urad Dal - 1 Cups
Salt - To Taste
Fenugreek Seed - 1 tsb
Edible Oil - 2-3 tbsps

Idly

Procedure

  1. Wash and Soak Rice and Dal in separate bowl minimum 6 hours.Next day drain both things.
  2. Add fenugreek if you like and grind rice and dal into a thick paste. Don't grind into too liquid. The paste should be thick enough and must not fall easily from the spoon.
  3. Pour the mixture into a deep pan and keep for 5-6 hours to form into its consistency.
  4. Apply oil to the idli molds and add salt to batter before pouring the idli batter. Add water to the idli cooker or normal cooker and cover it with a lid.
  5. Cook for about 10 minutes. Check, if done take out from the cooker, else keep for another 5 minutes. Check with fork or knife.
  6. Your Spongy and Soft indian idly is ready to serve. Serve it with Coconut Chutney and Sambhar for awesome taste.
